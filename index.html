About Us

Coko Shop is a shop that provides a variety of children's clothing needs. Starting from a building located in Bogor, our store can finally reach a wider market through the internet. As technology evolves, we try to innovate in business

Coko Shop was founded by Coko in 2021. The existence of this online store is expected to make it easier for you to get a variety of high quality products but at an affordable price. In providing services, we always try to give the best offerings to anyone.
